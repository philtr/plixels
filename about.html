History

On November 7, 2004, Grant "Splorp" Hutchison posted plixels.com on his infamous Available Domain of the Week page. By the time I found it, someone had already taken it. But that was OK, because I preferred plixels.net, and registered that instead.

At some point in the mid-2010s, I let the domain expire and nearly forgot about it. Well, we're back, baby, and BETTER THAN EVER! And, as of August 14, 2024, I also now own plixels.com!
